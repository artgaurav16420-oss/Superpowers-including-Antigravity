# Botanical Garden

A fresh and organic theme featuring vibrant garden-inspired colors for lively presentations.

## Color Palette

1. **Fern Green**: `#4a7c59` - Rich natural green
1. **Marigold**: `#f9a620` - Bright floral accent
1. **Terracotta**: `#b7472a` - Earthy warm tone
1. **Cream**: `#f5f3ed` - Soft neutral backgrounds

## Typography

1. **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
1. **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Garden centers, food presentations, farm-to-table content, botanical brands, natural products.
