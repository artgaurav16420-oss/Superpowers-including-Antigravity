# Forest Canopy

A natural and grounded theme featuring earth tones inspired by dense forest environments.

## Color Palette

1. **Forest Green**: `#2d4a2b` - Primary dark green
1. **Sage**: `#7d8471` - Muted green accent
1. **Olive**: `#a4ac86` - Light accent color
1. **Ivory**: `#faf9f6` - Backgrounds and text

## Typography

1. **Headers**: FreeSerif Bold
1. **Body Text**: FreeSans

## Best Used For

Environmental presentations, sustainability reports, outdoor brands, wellness content, organic products.
