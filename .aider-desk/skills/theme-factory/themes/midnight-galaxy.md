# Midnight Galaxy

A dramatic and cosmic theme with deep purples and mystical tones for impactful presentations.

## Color Palette

1. **Deep Purple**: `#2b1e3e` - Rich dark base
1. **Cosmic Blue**: `#4a4e8f` - Mystical mid-tone
1. **Lavender**: `#a490c2` - Soft accent color
1. **Silver**: `#e6e6fa` - Light highlights and text

## Typography

1. **Headers**: FreeSans Bold
1. **Body Text**: FreeSans

## Best Used For

Entertainment industry, gaming presentations, nightlife venues, luxury brands, creative agencies.
