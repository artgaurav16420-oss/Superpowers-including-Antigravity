# Arctic Frost

A cool and crisp winter-inspired theme that conveys clarity, precision, and professionalism.

## Color Palette

1. **Ice Blue**: `#d4e4f7` - Light backgrounds and highlights
1. **Steel Blue**: `#4a6fa5` - Primary accent color
1. **Silver**: `#c0c0c0` - Metallic accent elements
1. **Crisp White**: `#fafafa` - Clean backgrounds and text

## Typography

1. **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
1. **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Healthcare presentations, technology solutions, winter sports, clean tech, pharmaceutical content.
