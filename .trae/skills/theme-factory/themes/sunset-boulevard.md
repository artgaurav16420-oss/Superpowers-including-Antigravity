# Sunset Boulevard

A warm and vibrant theme inspired by golden hour sunsets, perfect for energetic and creative presentations.

## Color Palette

1. **Burnt Orange**: `#e76f51` - Primary accent color
1. **Coral**: `#f4a261` - Secondary warm accent
1. **Warm Sand**: `#e9c46a` - Highlighting and backgrounds
1. **Deep Purple**: `#264653` - Dark contrast and text

## Typography

1. **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
1. **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Creative pitches, marketing presentations, lifestyle brands, event promotions, inspirational content.
