# What are Ornamental Crops?
## सजावटी फसलें क्या हैं?

![bg left:35%](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1466692476868-aef1dfb1e735?auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=800)

<div class="bilingual-grid">
<div>
<div class="lang-label">English</div>
<p>Ornamental crops are plants grown primarily for their <b>aesthetic beauty</b>, decorative purposes, and environmental enhancement.</p>
</div>
<div>
<div class="lang-label">Hindi</div>
<p>सजावटी फसलें वे पौधे हैं जिन्हें मुख्य रूप से उनकी <b>सुंदरता</b>, सजावटी उद्देश्यों और पर्यावरण को बेहतर बनाने के लिए उगाया जाता है।</p>

# Principles of Design
## लैंडस्केपिंग के सिद्धांत

<div class="bilingual-grid">
<div>
<div class="card"><b>Balance / संतुलन</b><br>Equal visual weight on both sides.</div>
<div class="card"><b>Proportion / अनुपात</b><br>Correct sizing relative to plants/buildings.</div>
<div class="card"><b>Rhythm / लय</b><br>Repetition of elements to create movement.</div>
</div>
<div>
<div class="card" style="border-left-color: #6a9bcc;"><b>Focal Point / मुख्य बिंदु</b><br>A feature that immediately draws the eye.</div>
<div class="card" style="border-left-color: #6a9bcc;"><b>Harmony / सामंजस्य</b><br>All parts blending together uniformly.</div>
</div>
</div>

## लैंडस्केप उपयोग

- **Architectural**: Creating Physical Boundaries & Screening.
- **Aesthetic**: Adding year-round color and interest.
